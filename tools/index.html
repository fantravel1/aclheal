Recovery Tools & Assessments

Free, validated screening tools to help you and your healthcare team monitor your mental health and psychological readiness throughout your ACL recovery journey.

Important Information About These Tools

These screening tools are for informational purposes only and are NOT diagnostic instruments. They cannot replace professional evaluation by a qualified healthcare provider. All responses are stored only on your device (localStorage) and are never transmitted to any server. If you're experiencing a mental health crisis, please call or text 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline) immediately.
