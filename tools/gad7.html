GAD-7 Anxiety Screening

The Generalized Anxiety Disorder 7-item (GAD-7) scale is a validated screening tool for assessing anxiety severity. Anxiety is common during ACL recovery—this tool helps you monitor your symptoms.

Important: This screening tool is for informational purposes only and is NOT a diagnostic instrument. It cannot replace professional evaluation. Your responses are stored only on your device and never transmitted to any server. If you're in crisis, call or text 988 immediately.
